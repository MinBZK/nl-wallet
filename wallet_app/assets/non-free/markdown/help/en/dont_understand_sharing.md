Data sharing means you can share your data without filling in a form or showing your physical card to an organisation. This helps you check and control what you share.

**What must you do?**

- Check which details are asked.
- Check if you want to share these details.
- Choose to share or to stop.
- Continue only if this feels right.
- Stop if you are not sure.
