Both can show an important message. They are not the same.

**What is a push notification?**

- A push notification is sent by your phone.
- You may see it outside the app.
- Tapping it opens the right page.

**What is a banner?**

- A banner is shown inside the app.
- It also opens the right page when you tap it.
- A banner does not send a push notification.
