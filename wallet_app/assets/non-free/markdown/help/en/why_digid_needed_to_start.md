You use DigiD to show it is you. This helps keep your wallet and your cards safe.

**What should you expect?**

- You leave NL Wallet for a moment.
- After you finish in DigiD, you come back to NL Wallet.
- Then you can continue.
- Your Personal data card and Address card will be created.

**What if you do not want to do this now?**

- You can stop and do it later.
- Until you do this step, NL Wallet is not ready to use.
