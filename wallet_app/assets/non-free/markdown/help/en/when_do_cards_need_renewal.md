Cards need replacement when they are no longer valid, or when the issuer gives you a new version.

**Which card can you replace in the app?**

- You can replace your Personal details card in the app.
- You do this with DigiD.

**Which cards are replaced outside the app?**

- Non-PID cards are replaced on the issuer website.
- You cannot replace these cards in the app.
