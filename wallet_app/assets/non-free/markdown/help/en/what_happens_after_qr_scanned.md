After your QR code is scanned, NL Wallet opens the next step.

**What happens next?**

- The other device reads your QR code.
- NL Wallet connects with that device.
- You then see what you need to do next.
- For example, you may need to check details or confirm something.

**What should you do?**

- Read the next screen.
- Continue only if the action is clear.
- Stop if you are not sure.
