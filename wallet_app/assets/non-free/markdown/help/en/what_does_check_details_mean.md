You can read the card details before you add the card.

**What should you check?**

- The card is from the right issuer.
- The card matches your details.
- The dates make sense.

**What if something looks wrong?**

- Do not add the card yet.
- Use "Are your details incorrect?" if that is shown.
- Contact the issuer if needed.
