After replacement, the new card is used in your wallet.

**What happens after replacing your Personal details card?**

- The new card is added in the app.
- You can continue in NL Wallet.

**What happens after replacing an Non-PID card?**

- You replace the card on the issuer website.
- Then the new card can become available in your wallet.
