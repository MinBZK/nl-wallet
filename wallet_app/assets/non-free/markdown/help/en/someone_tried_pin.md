Stop trying PINs if you think someone else used your wallet.

**What must you do?**

- Stop using random PINs.
- Wait until the block is over.
- Try your own PIN carefully.
- Tap "Forgot PIN?" if you are not sure of your PIN.
- Delete this wallet if you no longer trust this phone.
