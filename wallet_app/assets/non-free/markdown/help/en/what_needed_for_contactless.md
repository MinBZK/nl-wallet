You need a phone that supports contactless use.

**What do you need?**

- A phone with NFC
- The right action open in NL Wallet
- The other device close by
- Both devices ready for the same action

**What should you know?**

- If your phone does not support NFC, this will not work.
- If the devices are too far apart, the action may fail.
