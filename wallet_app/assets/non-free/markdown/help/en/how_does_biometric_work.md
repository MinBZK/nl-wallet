You can use face scan or fingerprint to open NL Wallet faster.

**What can you do?**

- Turn the switch on to use face scan or fingerprint.
- Turn the switch off to stop using it.
- Use your PIN if you prefer.

**How do you turn it on?**

- Turn the switch on.
- Enter your PIN to confirm.

**How do you turn it off?**

- Turn the switch off.
- You use your PIN after that.
