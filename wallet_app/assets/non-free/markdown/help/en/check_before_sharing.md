Check who asks, why they ask, and what you will share.

**What should you check?**

- The organisation name
- Why the details are needed
- The list of details
- The agreement if you want more information

**What can you do?**

- Tap "View" to see all details from a card.
- Tap "Read the agreement".
- Tap "Share" to continue.
- Tap "Stop" to cancel.
