After sharing, NL Wallet opens the next step.

**What happens next?**

- The organisation receives your details.
- You go back to the website or to your overview.
- You can also open the activity to check what was shared.

**What should you do?**

- Read the next screen.
- Continue only if the next step is clear.
- Stop if you are not sure.
