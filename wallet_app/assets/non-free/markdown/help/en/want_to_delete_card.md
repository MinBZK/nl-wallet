You can remove the card from your wallet.

**What must you do?**

- Open the card.
- Check if this card can be deleted.
- Choose "Delete card" if that option is shown.
- Check if you really want to remove it.
- Continue only if you are sure.

**Can you delete every card?**

- You cannot delete your Personal details card.
- This card stays in your wallet.
- Other cards may have a "Delete card" option.
