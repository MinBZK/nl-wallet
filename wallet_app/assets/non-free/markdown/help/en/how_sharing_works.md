You can share your details in a simple way. You stay in control.

**What should you know?**

- You check what will be shared.
- Nothing is shared until you enter your PIN.
- You see which details are requested.
- You choose to share or to stop.
- Nothing is shared until you agree with your PIN

**When can you use this?**

- When your NL Wallet is ready to use.
