The QR code starts a secure action with another device or organisation. For example, you use it when an organisation asks you to show your QR code.

**What can it be used for?**

- To share details
- To log in
- To start another action in NL Wallet

**What should you know?**

- The other side scans the QR code.
- Then NL Wallet opens the next step.
