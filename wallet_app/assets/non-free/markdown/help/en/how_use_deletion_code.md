You can use your deletion code in two ways.

**On the NL Wallet website**

- Go to the NL Wallet website.
- Enter your 18-character deletion code.
- Do the check step.
- Wait until you see that it worked.

**By phone**

- Keep your deletion code ready.
- Call the helpdesk.
- Ask the helpdesk to delete your NL Wallet.
- Say your 18-character deletion code.

**What happens next?**

- Your NL Wallet is deleted when your phone connects to the internet.
