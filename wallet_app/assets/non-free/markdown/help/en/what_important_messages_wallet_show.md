NL Wallet can show important messages by push notification or by banner.

**Examples**

- A message about your wallet
- A message about an important change
- A message that helps you continue safely

**What should you know?**

- A banner is shown in the app.
- A push notification is shown by your phone.
- A banner does not trigger a push notification.
