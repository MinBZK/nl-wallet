The card is removed from NL Wallet.

**What should you know?**

- You cannot use the card anymore.
- If you need it again, you must add it again.

**Can every card be deleted?**

- No.
- You cannot delete your Personal details card.
- Only cards with a "Delete card" option can be deleted.
