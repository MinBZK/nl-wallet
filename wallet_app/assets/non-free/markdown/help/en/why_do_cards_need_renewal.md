Cards need replacement so your wallet keeps the right and current card.

**Why is this important?**

- Old cards can stop working.
- A new card may be needed.
- This helps keep your wallet up to date.

**Important to know**

- Personal details card replacement is done in the app with DigiD.
- Non-PID card replacement is done on the issuer website.
