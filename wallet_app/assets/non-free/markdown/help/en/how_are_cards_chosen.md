You can choose which cards you want to add.

**What should you know?**

- All cards may be selected first.
- This helps you add cards faster.
- You can remove a card before you continue.

**What must you do?**

- Check which cards are selected.
- Remove cards you do not want.
- Continue with the cards you want to add.
