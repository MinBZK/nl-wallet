By tapping "Start your action" and then "Use contactless (NFC)".

**What do you need to do?**

- Tap "Start your action" from the app overview or from the menu.
- Select "Use contactless (NFC)".
- Hold your phone close to the other device.
- Keep both devices still for a moment.

**What should you know?**

- The phones must be close together.
- The action starts when the connection works.
- Then NL Wallet opens the next step.
