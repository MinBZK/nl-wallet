You use your PIN to show it is really you.

**Why is this important?**

- This helps keep your data safe.
- You need your PIN for important actions.
- For example, when you share data or move your wallet.

**What can you do?**

- Enter your PIN to continue.
- Tap "Forgot PIN?" if you do not remember it.
- Tap "Back" to go back.
- Tap "Stop" or "Close" to stop the action.
