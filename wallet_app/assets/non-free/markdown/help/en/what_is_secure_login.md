You decide first. Nothing happens until you choose.

**How it works**

- You see who is asking to log in.
- You choose one option.
- Enter your PIN to continue, or stop.

**Your control**

- You see the name first.
- You can stop before you enter your PIN.
- If you stop, the login stops.

**When can you use this?**

- When your NL Wallet is ready to use.
