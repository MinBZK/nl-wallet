Choose only the cards you want to add.

**What must you do?**

- Keep cards selected if you want to add them.
- Tap "Add" to select or unselect a card.
- Tap "Select all" when there is more than one card.
- Tap "Check details" to see more about a card.
- Tap "Stop" to cancel.
