**What must you do?**

- Wait a little longer.
- Keep the app open.
- Check your internet connection.
- If it still does not continue, stop and start again.

**What if you see "No internet" or "No response"?**

- Turn Wi-Fi or mobile data on.
- Try another connection.
- Try again later.

**What if you see "Device insecure"?**

- Update your phone.
- Make sure your phone has a screen lock.
- NL Wallet may not work on this phone if it is not supported.
