After you scan the QR code, NL Wallet opens the next step.

**What happens next?**

- NL Wallet reads the QR code.
- The app opens the right action.
- You then see what you need to do next.
- For example, you may need to check details or continue on another screen

**What should you do?**

- Read the next screen.
- Continue only if the action is clear.
- Stop if you are not sure.
