The demo is only an example. Nothing in the demo is real.

**What is it for?**

- The demo shows how NL Wallet works.
- You can use it to try the steps.

**What is not real?**

- The demo uses test data.
- The demo does not use your real cards.
- The demo does not use your real details.

**What can you do in the demo?**

- Tap through the steps.
- Try the buttons.
- Close the demo when you are done.

**What can you not do?**

- You cannot log in with real organisations.
- You cannot share real data.

**Is your use tracked?**

- During testing, the app may save what you do in the demo.
- This helps improve the app.
