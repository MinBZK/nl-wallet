Your wallet is blocked for a short time. Wait 10 minutes and try again.

**What must you do?**

- Wait 10 minutes.
- Then try your PIN again.
- Tap "Forgot PIN?" if you do not remember your PIN.
- Tap "Delete Wallet" only if you want to remove this wallet from this phone.
